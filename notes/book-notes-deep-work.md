# Deep Work - Cal Newport

## Core Thesis
Deep work = professional activities performed in a state of distraction-free concentration that push your cognitive capabilities to their limit.

## Rules
1. **Work Deeply** — Schedule deep work blocks, ritualize the process
2. **Embrace Boredom** — Resist the urge to switch to distraction at the first sign of boredom
3. **Quit Social Media** — Apply the craftsman approach to tool selection
4. **Drain the Shallows** — Schedule every minute of your day

## Key Takeaway
> "The ability to perform deep work is becoming increasingly rare at exactly the same time it is becoming increasingly valuable."
